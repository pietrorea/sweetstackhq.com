In-venue mobile ordering for your restaurant.

Turn your guest’s phone into an ordering platform. Make better use of your space, increase profits and level-up your service.

How does it work?

Step 1: Take photos

Take photographs of all items on your menu. Make these images as compelling as possible. If you already have images you can use, you can upload these.

Step 2: Upload menu

Sign up for an account. Enter the information for all your dishes, such as name, description and price. Upload the images you took in the previous step.

Step 3: Print QR code

Once you’re happy with your menu, we’ll generate a unique QR code for your restaurant. Print it and put it on every table, outside the front door, or wherever else you’d like.

Go beyond PDF menus...

Automatic translation

Your digital menu will automatically translate your text to 10 different languages. Your guests can switch to the language they prefer.

Mark items unavailable

Ran out of pork? No problem. Mark dishes unavailable to let guests know. Mark them them available again once you restock.

Unlimited edits

Need to raise prices? No problem. Edit anything about your menu 24/7. All your changes always show up in your unique QR code.

Menu scheduling

Surface different menus based on time of the day/week: happy hour menus after 5pm and brunch menus during the weekend.

Fully searchable

Got a big menu? Guests can take advantage of the full-text search to quickly find what they’re looking for.
